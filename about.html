About Kaushalya Desi Eggs

Kaushalya Desi Eggs is built on traditional farming knowledge. We raise Gavran hens with natural feed and free range practices to ensure the healthiest eggs and meat for your family. Founded by local farmer Bapurao Khairnar, our mission is to deliver premium, honest produce.
